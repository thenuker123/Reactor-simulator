NUCLEARES

Nuclear Reactor Simulator

You are the operator of a Pressurized Water Reactor (PWR) nuclear power plant. Your mission is to safely start the reactor, generate electricity, and supply power to the city.

Startup Checklist:

  1. Turn on External Power
  2. Activate Pressurizer heaters & thermostat
  3. Turn on Condenser Vacuum
  4. Load Fuel into the reactor
  5. Start Primary Coolant Pump (set to ~50%)
  6. Start Condenser Pump (~25%)
  7. Slowly withdraw Control Rods to increase power
  8. Once steam temp is above 100°C, start Secondary Pump
  9. Open MSCV to send steam to turbine
  10. Wait for turbine RPM to reach ~3000
  11. Use Synchroscope to synchronize, close Circuit Breaker
  12. Connect to city grid and supply power!
